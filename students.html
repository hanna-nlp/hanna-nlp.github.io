Students & Team

My current team consists of research scientists, research engineers, predoctoral young investigators, and Young Investigators at Ai2 working on OLMo and Tulu, along with my students and postdocs at H2Lab. As of 2025, I have successfully graduated 14 PhD students and 10 PostDocs.

Hiring and Recruiting: I am always looking to hire talented research engineers and scientists at Ai2 (check the active calls) and students and postdocs at H2Lab. If you are an undergraduate student and want to work with my lab, please fill out the questionnaire available on the H2Lab website.
